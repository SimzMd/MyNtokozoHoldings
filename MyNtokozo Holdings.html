Welcome to MyNtokozo Holdings

To add value in every project or solution, we provide to all our clients. We aim to become a major role player in the Electronic Security, Education and Information Technology sectors of the South African economy.

Our Mission

To design and provide solutions in Electronic Security, Education, and Information Technology that assist in service delivery improvement.

Business Overview

We aim to build an ongoing relationship that will provide a top-quality service by establishing a comfortable partnership that will support the needs of our clients and allow them more time to concentrate solely on their business and not get overwhelmed by the never-ending time-consuming tasks.

Our Values

  • PROFESSIONALISM – Competence and effectiveness in our work.
  • ACCOUNTABILITY – We are personally accountable for delivery of our commitment and promise, therefore we deliver, whatever it takes.
  • QUALITY – High standards are a way of life. We pursue excellence in everything we do.
